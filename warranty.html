Warranty FAQ

How long does warranty service take?

Most warranty claims are resolved within 7-10 business days from when we receive your controller. Express service may be available for urgent cases.

Do I need to keep my receipt?

While helpful, a receipt isn't always required. We can verify your purchase through your order number or product registration.

What if my controller is discontinued?

If your exact model is no longer available, we'll provide an equivalent or upgraded replacement at no additional cost.

Does warranty transfer to new owners?

Yes, our warranty follows the product. If you sell or gift your controller, the remaining warranty period transfers to the new owner.
