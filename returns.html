How to Return Your Controller

Simple steps to get your return started

1

Contact Us

Email us at returns@gamezonepro.com or call +1 (555) 123-GAME with your order number and reason for return.

  • Have your order number ready
  • Briefly explain the reason for return
  • We'll respond within 24 hours
2

Get Return Authorization

We'll send you a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and prepaid shipping label.

  • RMA number for tracking
  • Prepaid return shipping label
  • Return instructions and packaging tips
3

Package & Ship

Pack your controller in its original packaging and attach the return label.

  • Use original box and packaging
  • Include all accessories and manuals
  • Attach prepaid return label
4

Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days.

  • Inspection within 24 hours of receipt
  • Refund processed in 3-5 business days
  • Email confirmation sent

Return Conditions

To ensure a smooth return process, please make sure your return meets these conditions:

Eligible for Return

  • Controller is in original, unused condition
  • All original packaging, accessories, and manuals included
  • No physical damage or signs of wear
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Valid proof of purchase (order number)

Not Eligible for Return

  • Controllers damaged by misuse or accidents
  • Items returned after 30-day window
  • Controllers with missing accessories or packaging
  • Items purchased with special promotions (case-by-case basis)
  • Controllers with unauthorized modifications

Perfect Condition

Controller looks and functions like new, with all original packaging and accessories.

✓ Full Refund

Minor Issues

Small cosmetic issues or missing non-essential items (like manual).

Partial Refund

Damaged/Used

Significant wear, damage, or missing essential components.

Return Rejected

Refund Information

Credit/Debit Cards

3-5 business days

Refunds are processed back to your original payment method. The time it takes to appear in your account depends on your bank.

PayPal

1-2 business days

PayPal refunds are typically the fastest, appearing in your PayPal account within 1-2 business days.

Digital Wallets

1-3 business days

Apple Pay, Google Pay, and other digital wallet refunds are processed quickly, usually within 1-3 business days.

Important Refund Notes

  • Original Payment Method: Refunds are always processed to your original payment method
  • Shipping Costs: Original shipping costs are refunded for defective items or our errors
  • International Orders: Currency conversion fees are not refundable
  • Partial Refunds: May apply if items are returned in less than perfect condition
